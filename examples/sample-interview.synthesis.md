### 1. Participant Profile
- Role: Sole owner of a design agency
- Context: Seeking a modern alternative to an outdated bank dashboard
- Goal: Set up a team account with a new financial platform
- Prior experience: Familiar with document management (Dropbox)
- Note: Expressed frustration with current bank's outdated interface

### 2. Sentiment Arc
The participant started with optimism and curiosity, feeling excited about the platform's promise of a quick setup. This shifted to frustration and confusion as they encountered unexpected document requirements and verification issues. By the end, they expressed resignation and a desire to revert to their old bank, indicating a significant emotional downturn.

### 3. Themes
- **Unexpected Document Requirements**  
  The participant was caught off guard by the document list appearing mid-signup rather than upfront.  
  *"Why is this on step two? Why isn't this on the homepage?"*

- **Confusion About Beneficial Ownership**  
  The participant struggled to understand what a beneficial ownership declaration entailed.  
  *"I don't even know what some of these are."*

- **Frustration with Required Fields**  
  The participant felt the form was overly prescriptive and confusing with required fields they couldn't fulfill.  
  *"The form is asking for percentages and there's a field for secondary beneficial owner that says required, it's required but I don't have one."*

### 4. Frictions
- **Unexpected Document Requirements**  
  The participant was surprised to see the document list in step two rather than on the homepage.  
  **Severity:** Major  
  *"Why is this on step two? Why isn't this on the homepage?"*  
  **Triggered by:** Document list appearing mid-signup

- **Required Field Without Valid Option**  
  The participant encountered a required field for a secondary beneficial owner they couldn't fulfill.  
  **Severity:** Blocker  
  *"I don't have one, interviewer, what do you do?"*  
  **Triggered by:** Required field for secondary beneficial owner

- **Annoying Identity Verification Process**  
  The participant found the identity verification process frustrating and unclear.  
  **Severity:** Major  
  *"Annoying, the first two times it didn't tell me what was wrong."*  
  **Triggered by:** Unclear identity verification feedback
